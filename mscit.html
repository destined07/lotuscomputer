What is MS-CIT?
MS-CIT (Maharashtra State Certificate in Information Technology) is a popular IT literacy course offered by Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited (MKCL). It is designed to introduce individuals to essential computer skills and IT concepts, making them proficient in using technology for day-to-day tasks

Why Choose MS-CIT?
Government Recognized: MS-CIT is a government-recognized certification, adding value to your IT skillset. Comprehensive Curriculum: Covers fundamental concepts like MS Office, Internet usage, email, and more. Interactive Learning: Includes animated tutorials, practical exercises, and assessments. Career Advancement: Enhances employability and opens doors to better job opportunities.

What You Will Learn
Basics of Computers and IT MS Office Applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) Internet and Email Essentials Cybersecurity Basics Digital Transactions and Online Tools
